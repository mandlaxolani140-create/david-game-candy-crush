HOW TO PLAY
// DAVID AI CANDY CRUSH GUIDE //
👆 DRAG TO SWAP CANDIES
Watch the animated demo below
1
Press & hold any candy you want to move.
2
Drag left, right, up, or down into a neighbouring candy to swap them.
3
If the swap lines up 3 or more of the same candy in a row or column — they POP! 🎉
4
If no match is made, the candies snap back automatically. No move is wasted.
5
Chain reactions create COMBO bonuses — the score multiplier grows with every cascade!
