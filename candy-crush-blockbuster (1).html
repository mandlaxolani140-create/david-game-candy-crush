HOW TO PLAY
// DAVID AI CANDY CRUSH GUIDE //
👆 DRAG TO SWAP CANDIES
Watch the animated demo below
1
Press & hold any candy you want to move.
2
Drag left, right, up, or down into a neighbouring candy to swap them.
3
If the swap lines up 3 or more of the same candy — they POP! 🎉
4
Match 4 in a row to create a 🎁 Striped Candy — it clears an entire row or column!
5
Match 5 in a row to create a 🌈 Colour Bomb — swap it with any candy to clear ALL of that colour!
6
Match an L or T shape of 5 to get a 💥 Wrapped Candy that explodes twice!
⭐ SPECIAL CANDIES
🎁
Striped Candy
Match 4 in a line. Clears entire row (horizontal) or column (vertical). Huge score!
💥
Wrapped Candy
L or T shape match of 5. Explodes in a 3×3 area — TWICE for double damage!
🌈
Colour Bomb
Match 5 in a row. Swap with any candy to destroy ALL candies of that colour on the board!
✨
Combo Power
Combine two special candies for MEGA effects. Striped+Wrapped = 5-line clear!
